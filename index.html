Projects

Flagship Project · 2024–Present
★ IEEE Award · U.S. Navy Award · SwRI Engineering Award · AJAS Keynote

MARLIN

An autonomous surface vehicle for proactive drowning prevention — built under $500. Believed to be the first proprietary ASV at this price point capable of real-time human detection via computer vision on constrained hardware.

Uses a FOMO ML model on an ESP32-S3, with GPS path recording and playback, heading-based differential thrust, magnetometer navigation, and LiDAR obstacle avoidance. Recognized by SwRI, IEEE, and the U.S. Navy.
