Introduction

I am a San Antonio-based robotics researcher conducting independent and institutional research during my gap year. My work sits at the intersection of embedded systems, autonomous navigation, and real-time computer vision — focused on deploying capable autonomy in constrained, real-world environments.

Currently Software Lead for TRIPPY, an open-source planetary rover platform, under Dr. Kevin Nickels at Trinity University. Concurrently developing MARLIN, an autonomous surface vehicle for maritime rescue, mentored by Dr. Prasanna Kolar at Southwest Research Institute (SwRI). Upcoming work at Alt-Bionics involves applying vision-language-action models to robotic prosthetics.
